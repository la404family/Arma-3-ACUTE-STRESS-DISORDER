[b]Soldier, welcome to Sefrou-Ramal.[/b] Forget standard rules of engagement. Here, there is no defined frontline. No guaranteed safe zone. You are not fighting an army; you are fighting paranoia itself.

[b]1. THE ENEMY IS INVISIBLE[/b]
Forget distinct uniforms. The enemy blends into the crowd. Thanks to a dynamic conversion system, any civilian can turn into an insurgent at any moment. That harmless passerby watching you? They could pull a weapon and gun you down in a second.

[*] [b]Constant Moral Dilemma:[/b] Shooting first is a war crime. Waiting for positive ID can be fatal. Do you have the nerves for this?

[b]2. THE TERRAIN IS MINED[/b]
Never lower your guard. The ground itself is your enemy. Anti-personnel mines are scattered randomly across the roads of the map.

[b]3. UNPREDICTABLE CHAOS[/b]
The mission generates its own emergencies. You don't choose the timing; you endure the chaos of the battlefield:
[list]
[*] [b]Civilian Protection:[/b] Protect the population amidst the crossfire. Collateral damage is not tolerated. (Global mission)

[/list]
